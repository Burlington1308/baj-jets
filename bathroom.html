Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install accessories on tiles without cracking them?

Use a carbide or diamond-tipped tile bit, turn off the hammer mode on your drill, and place masking tape over the drilling point to prevent the bit from slipping.

Do you offer installation services for all bathroom accessories?

Yes, professional fitters can install accessories like towel rails, toilet roll holders, robe hooks, soap dishes, shower caddies, mirrors, and lighting.

What are the best materials for durable fittings?

Stainless steel and brass are recommended for their corrosion resistance and durability in moisture-heavy environments.

How do I know if my wall can support heavy accessories?

A, professional fitter will perform a sound test to determine if the wall is solid brick or plasterboard, then use appropriate rawl plugs and fixings.

What is the difference between bathroom fixtures and fittings?

Fixtures are fixed in place and usually connected to plumbing or electricity (e.g., toilets, sinks, bathtubs). Fittings are more flexible, often added onto surfaces or walls, and can be easily swapped or moved (e.g., towel rails, soap dispensers, toilet roll holders).
