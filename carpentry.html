Frequently Asked Questions

Do you nail or glue dado rails?

An solvent free adhesive would be fine, nails could be used to help support the dado whilst the glue sets.

Are the polystyrene cornices waterproof and mould resistant and can they be used in a bathroom?

Yes, our polystyrene cornices are waterproof and mould resistant, making them suitable for bathroom use.

Can you paint over wooden skirting boards?

Yes, wooden skirting boards can be painted to match your interior decor.

Why should I choose wall panels over paint or wallpaper?

Wall panels add texture and depth to a room, providing a more dynamic and visually appealing finish compared to paint or wallpaper.
